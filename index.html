01 / About

About Me

I hold computer science degrees and have more than eight years of experience working with IT systems in FDA-approved pharmaceutical and laboratory environments. My core expertise lies in developing AI agents, building Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) frameworks, database administration, and validating computerized systems to meet US-FDA 21 CFR Part 11, Annex 11, WHO, UK MHRA, AUS TGA, and GxP regulations.

I have developed multiple internal applications and AI-based tools to support documentation, data management, and process optimization. Working alongside pharmacists, chemists, and quality teams has deepened my understanding of technology's role in healthcare.

These experiences have focused my research toward medical engineering, health informatics, and healthcare AI — particularly in designing data-driven systems that make clinical and laboratory practices safer and more efficient.
